## Overview
Lumen is a master of light beams who tethers enemies with persistent damage over time. This unique character brings a tactical "chain damage" playstyle to ShapeSlayer.

## Character Stats
- **Name**: Lumen
- **Icon**: ðŸ’¡
- **Health**: 75 HP (4 hearts)
- **Speed**: 4.3
- **Traits**: Marksman + Speed Demon
- **Starting Weapon**: Lumen Beam

## Weapon: Lumen Beam

### Mechanics
- **Projectile Damage**: 0.3 damage on impact
- **Beam Damage**: 0.1 damage per 0.1 seconds (1 DPS per beam)
- **Max Beams**: 3 simultaneous tethers
- **Beam Behavior**: When hitting a 4th enemy, removes oldest beam
- **Ammo**: 3 shots
- **Reload Time**: 2 seconds (medium)
- **Projectile Speed**: 10 (semi-fast)

### Gameplay
1. Fire projectiles at enemies to create light beam tethers
2. Tethered enemies take continuous damage (1 DPS per beam)
3. Maintain up to 3 beams simultaneously
4. Beams persist until target dies
5. Strategic targeting: older beams are removed when hitting 4th target

### Visual Effects
- **Yellow light beams** connecting Lumen to enemies
- **Pulsing animation** for active beams
- **Glow effect** around beam connections
- **Ammo bars** above character (Brawl Stars style)

## Installation
1. Enable the mod in the Mod Manager (M key)
2. Select Lumen from character selection screen
3. Start playing!

## Tips
- **Multi-target**: Spread beams across multiple enemies for maximum DPS
- **Focus Fire**: All 3 beams on one tanky enemy for burst damage
- **Positioning**: Stay mobile to maintain line-of-sight with tethered enemies
- **Reload Management**: 3 ammo means you can quickly establish all beams then reload

## Balance Notes
- Total DPS: 3 DPS (with 3 active beams) + projectile damage
- Lower direct damage but strong sustained DPS
- Requires tactical target selection and positioning
- Medium reload speed provides balance between Pierce Sniper and Toxic Spray
